Weavers Tent

at Remembering the Village

More information will be avaliable in the coming weeks.

Processing Cattails for Weaving

Friday morning and afternoon there will be a short demonstration in the weavers tent on how to harvest and dry cattails for weaving. This is a fun family friendly activity, all are welcome to join.

Weaving a Cattail Berry Basket

After each processing demonstration there will be two sessions where you will have the opportunity to weave a small berry basket. Each session will be limited to 6 participants, so we can all fit in the tent if it is raining.

Weaving a Small Willow Heart

On Saturday there will be two sessions where you will have the opportunity to weave a small willow heart. Each session will be limited to 6 participants, so we can all fit in the tent.
