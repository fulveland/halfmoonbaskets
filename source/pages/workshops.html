Host a Basketry Workshop with Half Moon Baskets

I’m thrilled that you’re considering hosting a basketry workshop. Below you’ll find my current offerings and availability.

I’m based near Lacombe, Alberta, and can teach within roughly 150 km of Lacombe most of the year.

In January, May, June, September, and October, I am open to traveling across Alberta to bring workshops to you.

During November, December, February, March, and April, I spend time harvesting near Calgary and Edmonton, and I’m available to teach workshops near those cities.

The price of my workshops reflects the immense amount of work involved in offering weaving classes in Alberta. There are no willow farms here — every stick used in my workshops is harvested by me, either from the wild or from willow I am helping to grow.

If you’re ready to share the joy of weaving with your friends, colleagues, or community, I would love to help you make it happen. Please reach out at halfmoonbaskets@gmail.com

Willow Wreath

One of the hardest things to make with willow is a wreath. It takes time to develop the intuition needed to bend it from a straight whip into a perfect circle. Would you like to try your hand at this ancient holiday tradition?

Details: Participants will recieve enought willow to make four 8-12” wreaths. We will make three in the workshop and they will have the opportunity to take the fourth bundle home if they would like.

Only avaliable from Nov 1 - March 31

Price: 115

Min: 6

Max: 30

Moon and Star

During this workshop, you will be invited to explore the many qualities of willow and how it can be worked with in basketry. We will talk about where to find willow in Alberta, how to harvest it responsibly, and ways to prepare it for weaving. You will also learn methods for drying, storing, and soaking willow so it is ready for future projects. By the end of the session, you will have created your own moon and star to take home and gained a deeper understanding of willow and its place in the weaving tradition.

Details:You can choose to have participants make a moon and star during the same workshop, or you can run two consecutive workshops one after the other. With a collections of stars being created in the first workshop and a larger moon being created in the second workshop.

Price: 115

Min: 6

Max: 20

Berry Boat

A boat of willow knows many voyages. It can wander into the brambles to gather berries, sit on the table holding crackers, or rest quietly on a shelf cradling tiny treasures. In this workshop, we will begin with a short tutorial to guide you in using a small jig to weave your own berry boat. Over two to three hours, your hands will coax the boat into shape, and by the end, you will hold a vessel that is ready for whatever adventures await you.

Price: 115

Min: 6

Max: 20

Suet Bird Feeder

Cattail Berry Basket

​What if the rustling cattails that line the marshes could be coaxed into carrying your berries home? ​Join us to find out, as we transform these green blades into a small vessel for gathering summer’s sweetest moments. ​In this hands-on workshop, you will learn how to weave cattails over a wooden form, practicing timeless techniques such as the checkerboard weave, pairing, and waling. Together, we will finish the basket with a simple trac border, creating a sturdy, beautiful berry basket made entirely from cattails.

Price: 90

Min: 8

Max: 20

Details: This project works beautifully for large groups. I currently have enough tools and supplies to host up to 40 participants at a time. For groups larger than 20, I would bring in another weaver to assist so everyone gets the support they need.

Textured Willow Bowl

Learn to weave a textured bowl using a rope coil weave. We'll start with a 3x3 slath base, then weave a 4-rod drop-down wale to create a slight foot before moving up the sides with the rope coil weave. Once you get the hang of it, this weave moves quickly. The basket will be finished with a rod border.

Price: 185

Min: 4

Max: 8

Large Round Willow Basket

Over two days, we will weave a generous round willow basket, learning the core techniques that form the foundation of stake-and-strand basketry. You’ll work with a 3 x 3 slath base, pairing, waling, and French randing. The basket will be finished with a classic rod border and optional inset handles.

This workshop offers the time and space to work deeply with willow, to practice the rhythms of weaving, and to carry home a basket built to serve for years to come.

Price: 275

Min: 4

Max: 8

Large Twisted Willow Basket

Join Freyja Ulveland for two full days of weaving as she guides you through the making of a beautiful, sculptural willow basket. This project is big, bold, and deeply satisfying to create — a basket that shows off the natural strength and beauty of willow. Together, we will work with generous bundles of material to shape a basket with a striking twisted rope effect.

Along the way, you will learn foundational techniques such as paring, waling, and rod borders, with the option to add a sturdy handle if you choose.

Price: 325

Min: 4
